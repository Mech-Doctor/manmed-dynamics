Automated Patient Series Detector & Digital Prescription

This system automatically detects recurring patient visits across departments using OCR, metadata parsing, and fuzzy logic matching. It connects test results, case notes, and radiology findings to a unified patient identity.

The tool then generates a clean digital prescription with embedded AI insights, enabling continuity of care across specializations and reducing duplication. Integrated with Canon multi-function scanners and SQL backends, it's tailored for Indian hospital workflows.
