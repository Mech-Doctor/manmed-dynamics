Ophthalmology AI Integrated Slit Lamp

This AI-powered slit lamp system is designed to assist ophthalmologists in real-time diagnosis during eye examinations. It captures live feed from a digital slit lamp and applies deep learning models to detect cataracts, diabetic retinopathy, and corneal diseases.

With edge-based inference and classification overlays on live footage, the system helps doctors make faster, more accurate assessments without manual image uploads.
