Radiology AI System

The Radiology AI System is built to automate interpretation of radiology reports such as X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs. It extracts structured findings, identifies abnormalities, and translates free-text inputs into consistent, searchable diagnostics.

This system is particularly valuable in high-volume hospitals where radiologists face bottlenecks in reporting. By using NLP and deep learning, it ensures improved triage, timely flagging of critical findings, and reduced reporting turnaround.
