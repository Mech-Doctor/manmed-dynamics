Telesurgical Robot

The Telesurgical Robot is a ROS2-controlled robotic arm designed for remote surgery assistance and incision mapping. It integrates multiple sensor modalities including LiDAR, ToF, and ultrasonic sensors to measure wound depth and geometry.

The robot captures high-resolution images mid-scan and feeds live data to an edge device. It is manually controlled via a joystick, with built-in automated linear scanning routines. Its applications range from wound assessment to precision surgical planning in underserved areas.
