Computer Vision for Dermatology

The Dermatology Vision System uses high-resolution camera imaging combined with convolutional neural networks (CNNs) to detect and classify skin lesions. It supports early identification of melanoma, acne, vitiligo, and other common dermatological conditions.

Built for outpatient use, this system provides real-time diagnostics and structured reports, reducing misdiagnosis and improving access in low-resource skin clinics.
