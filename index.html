About Us

Manmed Dynamics is a research-driven medtech company building the next generation of intelligent surgical and diagnostic systems directly from inside hospital operating rooms. Founded by Vishwa, a mechatronics engineer and visiting researcher at MIMS, our mission is to bridge the gap between medicine and engineering through human-centered innovation.

From detecting microscopic cancer residues to guiding robotic surgeries and interpreting diagnostics with AI — our projects redefine what's possible in medicine. Each system is developed hand-in-hand with doctors and validated in real operating theatres to ensure real-world reliability and clinical relevance.

At Manmed Dynamics, we don’t just build prototypes — we engineer intelligence that saves lives.
